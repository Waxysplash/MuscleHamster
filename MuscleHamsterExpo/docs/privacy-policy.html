Privacy Policy

Muscle Hamster App • Last Updated: March 3, 2026

Summary: Muscle Hamster is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only what's needed to make the app work, we don't sell your data, and you can delete your account anytime.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Muscle Hamster! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Muscle Hamster, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address – Used for account login and recovery
  • Display name – Your chosen name shown in the app
  • Authentication data – If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and your email from those services

2.2 Profile Information

During onboarding, you provide:

  • Age confirmation – We verify you are 13 or older (we do not store your exact birth date)
  • Fitness preferences – Fitness level, goals, workout frequency preferences
  • Hamster name – The name you give your virtual pet

2.3 Activity Data

As you use the App, we collect:

  • Workout completions – Which exercises you complete and when
  • Exercise journal entries – Weights, reps, sets, and notes you log
  • Points and streaks – Your earned points and streak progress
  • Shop purchases – Virtual items purchased with in-app points
  • Workout feedback – Your ratings of workouts (loved/liked/not for me)

2.4 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and OS version – To ensure app compatibility
  • App version – To provide appropriate features and support
  • Crash reports – To identify and fix bugs (no personal data included)

2.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or camera data
  • Health data from Apple Health or Google Fit
  • Biometric data
  • Financial or payment information

3. How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose
Account info Enable login, sync data across devices, account recovery
Fitness preferences Personalize workout recommendations
Activity data Track your progress, calculate streaks and points
Exercise journal Show your workout history and progress over time
Technical data Improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

  • Cloud storage: Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, hosted in the United States
  • Local storage: Some data is cached locally on your device using encrypted storage (expo-secure-store)

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Local data is encrypted using your device's secure storage
  • Firebase credentials are secured via environment variables
  • Sensitive data (passwords, tokens) are never logged or stored in plain text

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We never have and never will sell your information to third parties.

5.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may process your data:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google Firebase Authentication, database, analytics Firebase Privacy
Apple Sign In Optional account authentication Apple Privacy
Google Sign In Optional account authentication Google Privacy

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access Your Data

You can view your profile information, activity history, and exercise journal directly in the App.

6.2 Update Your Data

You can edit your profile, fitness preferences, and hamster name in the App's Settings.

6.3 Delete Your Data

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. Upon deletion:

  • Your account will be permanently removed
  • All workout history, journal entries, and progress will be deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

6.4 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us. We will provide your data in a machine-readable format within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Muscle Hamster is intended for users aged 13 and older. We implement an age gate during sign-up to verify this.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backups: Backups are automatically purged after 90 days

9. International Users

9.1 European Union (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Our legal basis for processing your data is your consent (when you create an account) and legitimate interest (to provide and improve the App).

9.2 California (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • We may notify you via the App or email for material changes
  • Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance
