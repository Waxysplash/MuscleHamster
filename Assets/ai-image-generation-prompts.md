## Tips for Consistency in ChatGPT

1. **Start a dedicated ChatGPT conversation** just for Muscle Hamster art — don't mix with other tasks
2. **Generate Asset #1 first** and regenerate until you love it
3. **Upload Asset #1 as reference** for every subsequent prompt: "Match the exact art style of this reference image"
4. **Do one batch at a time** — finish all adult states before moving to baby
5. **If style drifts**, re-upload the original reference and ask ChatGPT to correct
6. **Check transparent backgrounds** — if ChatGPT adds a background, say "Remove the background, make it fully transparent PNG"
7. **Save originals at high res** — you can always scale down, but can't scale up
